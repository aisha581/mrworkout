## The Corporate Fitness App Experience

Let's be honest about what most fitness apps are selling you.

### Soft Metrics for Soft Results

Standard apps measure your "wellness score." They celebrate you for showing up. They send push notifications at 7:30 AM that say things like *"Hey champion! Ready to crush it today? 💪"*

You tap dismiss. You go back to sleep. Nothing changes.

These platforms are designed around **retention**, not **results**. They need you engaged, not transformed — because a transformed athlete doesn't need a $14.99/month subscription to feel motivated.

### The Notification Industrial Complex

Corporate fitness apps average 4–7 push notifications per day. Studies show this creates a dopamine loop that makes users *feel* productive without doing any actual training.

You're not building muscle. You're building a relationship with a notification.

### One-Size-Fits-Nobody Programming

Generic "beginner," "intermediate," and "advanced" tracks written by committee. The same squat tutorial you could find on YouTube in 2014. No adaptation. No science. No edge.
